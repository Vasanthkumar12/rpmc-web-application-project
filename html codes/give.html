GIVING

GIVING the Lord said "It is more blessed to give than to receive(Acts 20:35 KNJV). Christian giving is sowing (2Cor 9:6-8) the principle is what we sow we shall reap, It's the Lord's promise that he would multiply what we sow we shall reap, It's the Lord's promise that he would multiply what we sowed not add to it (2 Cor 9:10). Our giving for His work glorifies God (2 Cor 9:13).

1) Giver: You can invest into the Lord's work by giving as the Lord leads you cheerfully one time or when you are inspired.

2) Partnership in the gospel work: you can be a partner in praying along with us and serve the Lord with us as investing into the mission work on regular basis every month (Phil 1:3-6)
